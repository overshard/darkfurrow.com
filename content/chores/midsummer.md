---
season: midsummer
section: the work this season asks for
---

- water in the morning, before the sun is high. evening watering invites fungus.
- harvest everything on time. overripe vegetables tell the plant to stop producing.
- pull spent crops and plant fall seeds in their place. bush beans, beets, carrots.
- stake and tie anything that is leaning. the weight of fruit will snap a stem.
- check the compost and turn it. midsummer heat makes it cook fast.
- order garlic for fall planting now. the good varieties sell out early.
- take notes on what worked and what failed. your memory will lie to you by winter.

midsummer work is relentless but honest. the garden is giving you everything it has. meet it halfway.

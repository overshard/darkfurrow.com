---
season: midsummer
section: what the stars are showing
---

- the summer triangle dominates: vega, deneb, altair, high and bright
- the milky way runs through it, visible on clear dark nights away from town
- scorpius is at his highest, the curved tail sweeping low in the south
- sagittarius follows scorpius, his teapot shape pointing toward the center of the galaxy
- cygnus the swan flies down the milky way, deneb at his tail
- the perseid meteors begin in late july, peaking in august, bits of comet swift-tuttle

the milky way was called the "backbone of night" by some old cultures. in midsummer it arches overhead like a bridge from horizon to horizon. go somewhere dark and look up.
